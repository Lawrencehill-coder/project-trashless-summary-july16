The Opportunity

Agvisors holds a $7M LOI on the former Rio Grande Valley Sugar Growers mill site in Santa Rosa, Texas — 608 acres with existing mill infrastructure, rail access, and heavy industrial capacity. The concept, Project Trashless, converts the site into a zero-waste recycling campus (shingles, tires, plastics) paired with a senior RV/cottage-home resort on the agricultural acreage.
